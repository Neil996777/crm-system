# Out Of Scope

Items in this file are not part of the committed P0/P1 v1 release unless the
project sponsor formally promotes them. P0/P1 items can only be removed from
scope through `Formal Scope Change by User`.

| ID | Item | Reason | Status |
|---|---|---|---|
| OOS-001 | Contract approval workflow | v1 P0 contract management is record/status/amount/linkage/attachment-or-notes only. Approval workflow is a later workflow enhancement. | Accepted out of scope for G8 Baseline |
| OOS-002 | Electronic signature | Requires external integration, legal workflow decisions, and provider selection. | Accepted out of scope for G8 Baseline |
| OOS-003 | Contract template generation | Requires template governance and document generation rules beyond v1 record management. | Accepted out of scope for G8 Baseline |
| OOS-004 | Quote approval and discount approval workflow | Approval policies are not yet defined and are not required for the first complete CRM loop. | Accepted out of scope for G8 Baseline |
| OOS-005 | Invoice management | Payment tracking is P0; invoice lifecycle is a separate finance process. | Accepted out of scope for G8 Baseline |
| OOS-006 | Email and calendar synchronization | Useful enhancement, but not required for the P0 CRM loop. | Accepted out of scope for G8 Baseline |
| OOS-007 | Advanced analytics, forecasting, and sales performance reporting | Basic manager overview is P1; advanced analytics are post-v1. | Accepted out of scope for G8 Baseline |
| OOS-008 | External integrations with Feishu, DingTalk, WeCom, ERP, or finance systems | Integration targets and contracts are not defined for v1. | Accepted out of scope for G8 Baseline |
| OOS-009 | AI sales summaries, next-step suggestions, and risk hints | Not required for v1 production validity. | Accepted out of scope for G8 Baseline |
| OOS-010 | Dedicated mobile app | Responsive behavior may be defined later, but a separate mobile app is not part of v1. | Accepted out of scope for G8 Baseline |
| OOS-011 | Multi-tenant SaaS organization management | Current discussion confirms team collaboration, not multi-tenant SaaS. Must be confirmed before architecture. | Accepted out of scope for G8 Baseline |
| OOS-012 | Complex contract version management | v1 contract management records core contract information and history; full versioning is deferred. | Accepted out of scope for G8 Baseline |
| OOS-013 | Multi-currency, tax, and discount automation | Amount policy has not been confirmed for v1 and requires finance/business rules. | Accepted out of scope for G8 Baseline |
| OOS-014 | Automatic lead assignment rules | Manual assignment is part of P0; automated assignment is a later automation capability. | Accepted out of scope for G8 Baseline |
| OOS-015 | XLSX import/export | CSV is the committed v1 P1 import/export format. | Accepted out of scope for G8 Baseline |
| OOS-016 | Email, SMS, or chat reminder delivery | In-app reminders are the committed v1 P1 reminder channel. | Accepted out of scope for G8 Baseline |
| OOS-017 | Contract attachment upload as a required P0 path | Contract notes are P0 required; attachment upload can be considered later. | Accepted out of scope for G8 Baseline |
